Gabryel
Salle-Hount

Photographer and visual director based in Paris. Specialising in fashion editorial, portraiture and campaign work, Gabryel's practice is defined by a raw sensitivity — images that hold tension between stillness and movement, the composed and the accidental.

Clients and collaborators have included emerging designers, independent magazines and cultural institutions across Europe. His approach treats every frame as a conversation — unhurried, precise, instinctive.
